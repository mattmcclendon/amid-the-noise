There’s something beautiful about letting people exist as they are —  
unedited, uncorrected, uncontained.

Someone mispronounces a word.  
Gets carried away talking about a show they love.  
Lights up over something that doesn’t make sense to you —

That’s joy in its rawest form.

You don’t have to understand it.  
You just have to let them have their moment.

Let them shine — even if their light isn’t yours.
